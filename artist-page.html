Min Sheng Hua

Biographie

Min Sheng Hua is a chinese photographer,. She is born in 1979 in Beijing. Mostly known for her black and white photographs, we can easily recognize the style of Min Sheng Hua with the light balance and the contrasts she uses in her photographs. Discover her work : womens' portrait which is called Female Trouble in 2015. She is inspired and gives her point of view about gender question.

Exhibition

Min Sheng Hua's photographs Female Trouble will be exposed from the 9th of May until the 16th of July 2017 at in Artist's Eyes Galery..
